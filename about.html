About Us

The Coffee Break team consists of a rotating group of coffee enthusiasts across the Manchester area, predominantly made up of university students. We believe that the best place to bond with someone is over some good coffee, whether homemade or in a shop, and we're here to provide you the best places to do so! Click on our coffee near me tab to find out places near you, or our reviews tab to find out our take on new places to go to!
